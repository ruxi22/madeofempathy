# madeofempathy

This is a small space shaped by what I keep returning to: empathy, vulnerability, and the quiet distance that sometimes grows between people.

I think many connections don’t disappear all at once. They fade, slowly, in the absence of understanding. And empathy, in its simplest form, might be what brings them back.

I’m working on a virtual reality experience that tries to translate what is hard to explain, how it feels to lose clarity, control, or familiarity in Alzheimer’s and Parkinson’s. Not as information, but as something you can briefly inhabit.

This project is not about accuracy in a clinical sense. It is about experience. About creating a moment where something usually invisible becomes felt, even if only for a short time.

I don’t want to build things that are only functional. I want to build things that stay with people.

---

## What this repository is

A personal space where I explore:

- empathy through immersive experiences
- human-centered design in virtual reality
- the intersection between technology and emotional understanding

---

## Current work

- virtual reality experience focused on Alzheimer’s and Parkinson’s
- built with Unity and XR interaction systems
- designed as an awareness tool, not a medical application

---

## Why this matters

Some things are hard to explain.  
Some experiences cannot be understood from the outside.

If technology can help us get even slightly closer to understanding each other, then it becomes more than just a tool.
